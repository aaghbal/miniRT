Reclaimed Oak with Hangup Seamless Wood Texture

$4.99

This rustic white oak texture showcases a beautiful array of grain patterning with subtle remnants of sandblasting medium used during the reclamation process (hangup). It’s a perfect fit for interior visualization or furniture rendering projects that are aspiring for a modern reclaimed look. It’s rustic, it’s modern, it’s unique, and it’s 100% tileable for easy integration into your existing scene files!

Description

Description

This seamless wood texture features an inspiring reclaimed oak veneer with a subtle hangup patterning on the grain, leftover from sandblasting during the reclamation process. This white oak species also showcases subtle appearances of knotty structuring that exudes naturalist vibes. Slap this texture on furniture, picture frames, or even flooring to instantly add a sense of modern rusticism to your rendering scene. This texture is 100% seamless and includes several pre-optimized sizes of both diffuse and bump maps to ensure easy integration across a variety of workflows.
